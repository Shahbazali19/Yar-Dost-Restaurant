Welcome To Yar Dost Dinner Restaurant

Little Story

Yar Dost Dinner Restaurant is one of the favourite restaurants of Pakistani Peoples. The adornment is unique in its own style. Food was so delicious that it can’t be expound in words. The neon paintings on the wall add-up the joy at being there. At the entrance a dwarf with an axe welcomes you. The sitting area is good with colourful traditional furniture. We took a lot of pictures there to keep the memory of the time we spent there. I typify the restaurant in one of the best restaurants in Pakistan..

The first time when visited, I instantly felt the great welcome and smelled the delicious food. It has beautiful decor and looks very fashionable. It has a whole wall made by glass so that sunlight could stream in the room. In addition, sofas replaced chairs so that we can sit very comfortable. To our satisfaction, they have a lot kinds of food supplied to us.
